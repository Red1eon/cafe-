Our Story

Rooted in Passion,
Brewed with Purpose

The Third Space began with a simple belief: that coffee is more than caffeine; it's a ritual, a conversation starter, and a bridge between cultures.

We partner directly with farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices. Every bean is roasted in small batches on our vintage Probat, allowing us to coax out notes of dark chocolate, citrus, and toasted caramel.

"Coffee is a language in itself. We simply translate it into something unforgettable."

- Elena Voss, Head Roaster
